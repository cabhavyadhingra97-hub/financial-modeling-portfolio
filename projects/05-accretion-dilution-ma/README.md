## 🛠️ Skills Demonstrated

- **M&A Modelling:** Full merger model — purchase price, PPA, combined financials
- **EPS Accretion/Dilution:** Financing mix optimization (cash vs. stock vs. debt)
- **Purchase Price Allocation:** Goodwill calculation, intangible identification, step-up amortization
- **Synergy Analysis:** Revenue and cost synergy quantification and phasing
- **Sensitivity Analysis:** 2-way tables on premium vs. synergies and financing mix
- **Valuation:** Implied multiples, exchange ratio calculation, collar structures
