## 🏢 Business Overview

Reliance Industries Limited is India's largest conglomerate by market capitalization, operating across four major business segments:

| Segment | Revenue Contribution | Key Metrics |
|---------|---------------------|-------------|
| **O2C (Oil-to-Chemicals)** | ~55% | Refining throughput 70+ MMTPA |
| **Retail (Reliance Retail)** | ~20% | 18,000+ stores, 3.5Cr+ customers |
| **Jio Platforms** | ~15% | 450M+ subscribers, ARPU Rs. 181 |
| **Oil & Gas (E&P)** | ~5% | KG-D6 block production ramp-up |

## 📐 Model Architecture (13 Sheets)

| Sheet # | Sheet Name | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | **Company Overview** | Business description, segment mix, competitive positioning |
| 2 | **Historical Financials** | 5-year P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow (FY19–FY23) |
| 3 | **Revenue Build** | Segment-wise revenue projections (FY24E–FY28E) |
| 4 | **Cost Structure** | COGS, SG&A, D&A by segment |
| 5 | **Working Capital** | DSO, DPO, DIO analysis and projection |
| 6 | **Debt Schedule** | Loan amortization, interest expense, refinancing |
| 7 | **WACC Calculation** | Cost of equity, cost of debt, capital structure optimization |
| 8 | **Residual Income Model** | Book value of equity + PV of future excess returns |
| 9 | **FCFE / DCF Model** | Free Cash Flow to Equity, terminal value, equity value |
| 10 | **Comparable Companies** | Peer group: ONGC, IOC, BPCL, Jio + international peers |
| 11 | **Sensitivity Analysis** | WACC vs. growth rate matrix, bull/base/bear scenarios |
| 12 | **Football Field Chart** | Valuation range visualization across all methods |
| 13 | **Executive Summary** | Investment thesis, key risks, target price summary |
