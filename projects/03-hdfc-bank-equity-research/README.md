## 📋 Investment Thesis (3 Key Pillars)

### 1. 📈 Merger Synergies — HDFC Ltd. Integration
Post-merger with HDFC Ltd., HDFC Bank gains India's largest mortgage portfolio, creating cross-selling opportunities across 77M+ customers. Estimated Rs. 2,500 Cr synergy value by FY26.

### 2. 💰 NIM Resilience in Rising Rate Environment
HDFC Bank's CASA ratio of 47% acts as a natural hedge. NIM held at 4.0% through 250bps rate cycle — superior to peer average of 3.7%.

### 3. 🌐 Digital Moat & Retail Franchise
PayZapp 2.0 onboarding 1M+ users/month; #1 credit card issuer (17M+ cards); SmartHub Vyapar ecosystem for SME clients creates sticky, low-cost deposits.
